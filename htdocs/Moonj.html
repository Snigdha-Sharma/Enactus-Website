Project Moonj

Our most recent venture in collaboration with the initiative by MHRD and state government of Uttar Pradesh under 'One District One Product Scheme'. The project aims at empowering the indigenious Moonj based handicraft products which have been facing existential challenge over the years.
Motivation The Naini area of Allahabad has been recognised widely for its Moonj Craft. But with time iy has been losing its charm and today it is significantly far from the time of its glory. The market offers variety of products made from the moonj like basket(daliya in Hindi), coaster stand, bags, decorative items and more. Eco-friendly moonj products have the potential to do well in the national as well as international markets.
Goal The team aspires to look for taking he plunge into variuos aspects related to supply chain, marketing, promotion and technological R&D assistance to tackle the various challenges which have been holding back this art to achieve the glory it deserves.
